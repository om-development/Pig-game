# Pig-game

This is a famous game i tried cloning.
This easily is the hardest thing i ever tried.
It was mainly focused on javascript DM.
Here i used sessionStorage , eventListener , and wrote many other functions.

# To-Play

Click on this link
Enter player data or just press done (It will send ghost value).

There are 2 players
Each player has to roll a dice.
If they get anything beside 1 then they can either hold or roll again.
If they get 1 in their first roll, second roll, ...... any roll their turn will be over and they will earn 0 points.
Each time they roll other than 1 their roll points will be increased then the player can press hold to add the current points into their auctual score.
The game ends when the player press hold and they have more or equal to 100 score points.

# That's it

After the game finishes just reload the page and you can go one more round with the same data you provided first.
